Red Velvet Cupcakes

This red velvet (actually not red velvet because I was lazy and didn't look for an accurate picture) cupcake recipe yields soft, fluffy cupcakes with a rich chocolate flavor and a vibrant red color. Topped with a creamy and tangy cream cheese frosting, these cupcakes are perfect for any occasion and are sure to impress your guests.
